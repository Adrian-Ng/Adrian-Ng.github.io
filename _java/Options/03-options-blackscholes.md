### Black Scholes differential equation

$$
\frac{\partial f}{\partial t} + rS\cdot \frac{\partial f}{\partial S}+\frac{1}{2}\sigma^2\S^2\cdot\frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial S^2} = rf
$$
